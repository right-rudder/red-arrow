Flight Training Overview

Allow Red Arrow Flight Academy to guide you to becoming a pilot with our renowned flight training. Our program is meticulously designed to take aspiring pilots from the fundamentals to the heights of flight capability. Guided by our expert CFIs, you'll experience a blend of comprehensive ground school education and hands-on flying, ensuring a well-rounded understanding of the art and science of aviation.

From the thrill of your first solo flight to mastering advanced maneuvers, our step-by-step program instills confidence, knowledge, and the skills necessary to fly safely and competently. We would love to be the ones to make your flight dream a reality. Email us or stop by if you’re interested.

It All Starts with Your PPL

Becoming a certified pilot with Red Arrow Flight Academy, like many other achievements, has a process. Here's a breakdown of the key stages in your path to obtaining a Private Pilot Certificate (PPL):

  • Kickstart your flight training by enrolling in our Private Pilot Program. Begin the process by filling out our application form and emailing the required documents to info@flyredarrow.com. For in-person enrollment, bring a photo ID (driver's license) and proof of citizenship (passport or birth certificate).

  • Upon arriving at our office, our front desk team will assist you in completing additional paperwork and integrating you into our online system. You'll receive a flight kit, including a backpack with an online ground course and essential materials for your at-home training.

  • Once the paperwork is complete, we'll match you with an experienced CFI who will guide you through the remaining stages of the program. Your instructor will become your mentor, providing personalized instruction tailored to your learning style and pace.

  • Immerse yourself in our comprehensive ground school education. Learn the ins and outs of aviation, covering navigation, weather patterns, regulations, and more. This foundational knowledge is essential for a well-rounded understanding of flying

  • Transition from the classroom to hands-on flight training. Experience the feeling of taking control of the aircraft under the guidance of your instructor. Master basic maneuvers and gain confidence in handling the aircraft.

  • Achieve a significant milestone by completing your first solo flight. This transformative experience marks your growing independence and proficiency as a pilot.

  • Complete the FAA written exam, demonstrating your theoretical understanding of aviation. Prepare with the guidance of your instructor to ensure success.

  • Undergo a checkride with a Red Arrow instructor. This test assesses your ability to apply theoretical knowledge and execute practical flying skills.

  • Upon successful completion of the written exam and checkride, you'll be awarded your Private Pilot License. This certification opens doors to further advancements in your aviation journey.

Budget Considerations

As you embark on your aviation journey, understanding budget considerations is crucial. Red Arrow Flight Academy operates on a pay-as-you-go basis, eliminating the need for down payments or long-term contracts. Whether pursuing a Private Pilot License or advancing to a Commercial Pilot License, we would like to provide color as much as we can into what it can cost to engage in flight training. Please bear in mind that these are not always fixed prices as it takes some students longer than others to advance through training.

Estimated Flight Training Costs:

  • Private Pilot License: Estimated $12,000 to $18,000
  • Commercial Pilot License (with no prior experience): Approximately $84,000 for Private Pilot License, Instrument Rating, and Commercial License
  • Commercial Pilot License (with PPL): Estimated around $67,000 for Instrument and Commercial Ratings

Financial Support: As a Part 141 flight school, Red Arrow is uniquely positioned to accept those leveraging their Post-9/11 GI Bills. We also have partnerships with the COOL Program (open to Army/Air Force for financial sponsorship of up to $4,000) and Purdue Global Universities for students looking to pursue flight as a career. For other financing options, please take a look at our Financing page.

Pilot Career Paths

If it’s a career in flight you’re after, then we stand ready at Red Arrow to take you to those heights. Beyond the attainment of your Private Pilot License, our training programs open doors to a multitude of exciting career possibilities.

Commercial Pilot: Elevate your aviation career by pursuing a Commercial Pilot License. This certification gives you the skills and qualifications necessary to fly for compensation or hire. Whether you aspire to pilot commercial flights, work for airlines, or engage in other commercial aviation roles, the Commercial Pilot License is your gateway to a dynamic and lucrative career.

Flight Instructor: Share your passion for aviation and become a Certified Flight Instructor (CFI). With this certification, you have the opportunity to guide and mentor new students on their journey to becoming pilots. Red Arrow Flight Academy values the importance of experienced instructors, and achieving your CFI opens the door to a fulfilling career in aviation education.

Specializations in Aviation: Our training programs lay the foundation for specialization in unique sectors of aviation. Whether you're interested in aerial surveying, aerial photography, or other specialized roles, Red Arrow Flight Academy equips you with the skills and certifications needed to excel in these distinct and exciting career paths.

As you progress through our training programs, we are dedicated to providing you with the knowledge, hands-on experience, and certifications essential for pursuing your dream career.
