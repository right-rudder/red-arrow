How to Become a Pilot

It’s as easy as 1-2-3!

Embark on the exciting journey to becoming a pilot with Red Arrow Flight Academy by following these structured steps:

  1. Register Your Interest: Enroll in Red Arrow's Private Pilot Program to receive comprehensive knowledge and hands-on experience guided by seasoned instructors.
  2. Learn the Ins-and-Outs: Immerse yourself in hands-on flight training and Red Arrow's tailored ground school education, gaining expertise in navigation, weather patterns, and regulations.
  3. Pass the Exams: Successfully complete the FAA written exam, a practical flight test with a FAA Designated Pilot Examiner (DPE), and receive your Private Pilot License. This achievement marks the beginning of further advancements in your aviation evolution.

By choosing our structured approach to pilot training, you’ll soon be up in the air on your own, and closer to succeeding in your aviation goals than ever before.

Military/Rotor Transition Training

As the only Part 141 certified flight school in the region, we specialize in providing airplane pilot training programs for our military students.

Whether you are a military or a civilian helicopter pilot, you can easily transition into a fixed-wing aircraft with an add-on. We offer add-on training for Private Pilot, Instrument Rating, and Commercial Pilot.

Further military offerings:

  • Military/Rotor Transition Programs: Tailored for individuals adding fixed wing aviation qualifications to their helicopter military experience, these programs facilitate a seamless transition to civilian flight training.
  • Post 9-11 GI Bill® Accepted: Red Arrow proudly supports our military students by accepting the Post 9-11 GI Bill® and offering financial assistance to those eligible for educational benefits.
  • Financing Options with the COOL Program (Open to Army/Air Force): Credentialing Assistance under the COOL Program provides financial support for education, empowering military students to pursue their aviation aspirations with grants ranging from $1,000 to $4,000 annually.

International Students

For international students and foreign pilot licenses.

Red Arrow Flight Academy warmly welcomes international students too. Our International Program is thoughtfully crafted to provide a seamless experience for pilots from around the world.

Just as with our domestic program, we offer a range of comprehensive training programs, including Private Pilot, Instrument Rating, and Commercial Pilot certifications. Our expert instructors, state-of-the-art fleet, and proven training methodologies ensure that international students receive top-notch education, preparing them for success in flying. Additionally, for those with existing pilot licenses, our streamlined process for converting foreign licenses to FAA certifications opens the door to further career opportunities in the United States.

At Red Arrow, we understand the unique needs of international students. Our M-1 Visa and training request procedures have been designed to facilitate a smooth transition, allowing students to focus on their training without unnecessary bureaucratic hurdles. Join us at Red Arrow Flight Academy and start your journey to becoming a pilot, no matter where you’re from.
